Privacy Policy

1. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Location

2. Non-Personal Information

We do not collect any non-personal information either, such as usage statistics, device type, or game behavior. Your data stays completely on your device.

3. Advertising

This app may display ads through third-party ad providers such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect limited information (such as your device’s advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. This is handled according to their own privacy policies.

You can opt out of ad personalization from your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe data has been collected unintentionally, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties except for ad display purposes, managed under their respective privacy policies.

6. Your Control

You can stop all data usage by:

  • Turning off internet access during gameplay
  • Uninstalling the app at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer using the email provided on the app’s store listing.
